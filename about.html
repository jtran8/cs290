Our Company

Sunrise Consulting is an internationally recognized solar engineering services leader with more than a decade of experience and over 10GW of completed solar projects. We provide unsurpassed engineering expertise to our long-term customers including utilities, municipalities, corporates organizations, NGO's and EPC firms, while developing utility-scale projects in-house.

What We Do

We are your one-stop shop for solar energy professional services. Our team of professionals allow Sunrise to offer turnkey project solutions from land acquisition to commercial operation. Headquartered in Toronto, Canada with outposts in the USA, Europe, and Asia, we deliver local expertise crucial to your project.

Track Record

Sunrise brings over a decade of experience and an extensive portfolio of projects that together total more than 15,000MW of operational solar. Our projects span the Americas, Europe, and Asia and include everything from 100MW utility-scale projects to sub 1MW carports.

Our Mission

At Sunrise we've made building a sustainable energy future our priority. We are focused on bringing clean energy to the forefront by implementing quality commercial solar and utility-scale solar projects across the world. We believe in leaving our plant a better place for generations and generations to come.
