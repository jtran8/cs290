Planning

When it comes to planning a site for a solar utility project, Sunrise does not believe in cutting corners. The planning process requires a great deal of negotiation with local jurisdictions, utility companies, and governing agencies.

Our team works through the terms of Conditional Use Permits, Power Purchase Agreements, and Interconnection Agreements while also discussing and making determinations on such issues as: topography and boundary surveys, grading, stormwater mitigation, fire suppression planning, options for mounting systems, and the need for an O&M building.

Our planning services include:
  • Surveys, Studies, & Mapping
  • Stormwater Prevention Plan
  • Planning Agency Approvals
  • PPA Negotiations
  • Planning Board Meetings
  • Decommissioning Plans


Management

Sunrise will monitor your site - or portfolio of sites - daily to ensure that the system is performing to specifications. Our analysts also monitor for accelerated photovoltaic device and system degradation over time.

The Operations and Maintenance team performs regular onsite inspections, diagnostics and preventive maintenance to address any potential issues and ensure your system continues to perform as designed.

We also provide construction management, our experienced project managers will ensure your project is complete on time.

Our management services include:
  • Asset Management
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • Corrective Maintenance
  • Array Cleaning
  • Construction Management
  • Warranty Claims
